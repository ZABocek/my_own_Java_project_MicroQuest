Why MicroQuest is a good project idea

This app is intentionally designed to be more unique than a generic to-do list while still being realistic to build. It blends social features, content creation, and discovery in a way that gives you a strong portfolio story.

What users can do

  • Browse fun or useful short adventures
  • Create their own quests
  • Save favorites
  • Comment on quests
  • Track popular content on a leaderboard

Why PostgreSQL fits this app

PostgreSQL is free, powerful, and appropriate for larger datasets. As your number of users, quests, comments, and saves grows, it remains a much stronger foundation than a lightweight toy database.

Starter architecture

  • MVC controllers
  • Thymeleaf views
  • Service layer
  • Spring Data repositories
  • Dockerized PostgreSQL
  • Maven build
  • VS Code launch settings
